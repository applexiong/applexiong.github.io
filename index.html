Biography

I graduated from Gao'an Middle School of Jiangxi Province, Jiangxi, China, in 2015, received the B.Eng. degree in information engineering from Southeast University (SEU), Nanjing, China, in 2019, and am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the National Mobile Communications Research Laboratory (NCRL), Southeast University, Nanjing, China, supervised by Prof. Zaichen Zhang. My current research interests include channel modeling and performance analysis, RIS & UAV & V2X communications, hybrid far- and near-field communications, wireless communication and sensing, and deep learning.

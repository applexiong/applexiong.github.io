Biography

I graduated from Gaoan Middle School of Jiangxi Province, Jiangxi, China, in 2015, received the B.Eng. degree in information engineering from Southeast University (SEU), Nanjing, China, in 2019, where I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the National Mobile Communications Research Laboratory, Southeast University, Nanjing, China. My current research interests include wireless channel modeling, reconfigurable intelligent surface (RIS), AI-driven technologies, estimation, and wireless localization.
